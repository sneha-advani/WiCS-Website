Members

All women in Computer Science, Computer Engineering, Computational Biology, Digital Media Design, Networked & Social Systems Engineering, or interested in computer science is automatically a member of WiCS! This includes all majors, minors, (un)coordinated dual degrees, and any woman simply interested in learning more about WiCS. All WiCS members are always free to come to our open events.
