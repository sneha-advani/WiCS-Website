Members

All women in Computer Science, Computer Engineering, Computational Biology, Digital Media Design, Networked and Social Systems Engineering, or interested in computer science is automatically a member of WiCS! This includes all majors, minors, dual degrees, and any women simply interested in learning more about WiCS. All WiCS members are always free to come to our open events!

Get Involved

There are a lot of ways for you to get involved. The easiest is to come to our events! Throughout the year, we host professional development events like coffee chats and information sesions, social events like WiCS Brunches and Study Breaks, speaker events and discussion forums, and more. We also have Side Projects and Mentorship programs for younger women to get involved with the community and learn more about computer science, and we host an all-female hackathon, FemmeHacks, in the spring.
